Place brand assets here.

Suggested files:
  logo.svg or logo.png     — Velora Records wordmark / logo
  favicon.ico or favicon.png — Browser tab icon (32x32 or 64x64)
  og-image.jpg             — Open Graph / social share image (1200x630)

To use a logo image:
  1. Add your logo file here.
  2. In index.html, replace the text-based .nav__logo content with:
       <img src="assets/images/brand/logo.png" alt="Velora Records" height="32" />

To add a favicon:
  Add this inside <head> in index.html:
       <link rel="icon" href="assets/images/brand/favicon.ico" />
